## Welcome to LangQuest

**Every Language. Every Culture.**

LangQuest is an app where you can collect text and audio language data quickly. It's **local-first** - meaning you can work offline and then sync to the cloud when connected. You can **collaborate with teammates**, **translate**, and **validate** within the community.

### Our Vision

Our vision is that **every language would have access to the world's knowledge** and **every culture would be able to share its meaning with the world**.

## How to Collect Data

### Recording Mechanics

To collect audio data in LangQuest:

1. **Hold the button to record** - Press and hold the record button while speaking
2. **Slide to record** - Alternatively, slide the button to lock recording mode
3. **Speech detection** - The app uses speech detection to capture your voice clearly

Each recording creates a small asset that represents that specific piece of content (like a verse or story segment).

## Working with Bible Projects

### Navigating Bible Content

1. **Select a Bible project** from your project list
2. **Browse the books of the Bible** (Genesis, Matthew, etc.)
3. **Select a book** to view its chapters
4. **Create a quest** by clicking on a chapter

### Recording Bible Verses

When you enter a quest (chapter):

1. If there are **no assets yet**, you'll see an empty state
2. Click the **record button** to begin
3. **Hold to record** individual verses:
   - "Matthew chapter 1 verse 1"
4. **Slide to record** multiple verses continuously:
   - "Matthew chapter 1 verse 2"
5. Each recording creates a **separate asset** per verse or segment

## Exploring Public Projects

### Browsing Community Projects

From your project list, you can see **public domain projects** available to explore:

- These projects are **not owned by you** (no crown icon)
- You **have not been invited** to these projects
- You may be able to **view or explore** them
- You may have **limited editing capabilities** depending on the project settings

## Collaboration Features

### Working Together

LangQuest makes collaboration easy:

1. **Invite others to collaborate** on your projects
2. **Team members receive notifications** when invited
3. **Projects appear in their list** once they accept
4. **Work together** on collecting and validating language data

### Syncing to the Cloud

- Start working **locally** on your device
- **Sync to the cloud** when you have internet connection
- **Collaborate with teammates** through cloud-synced projects
- **Never lose your work** with automatic backup

## Tips for Success

- **Start small** - Begin with a single chapter or story
- **Practice recording** - Get comfortable with hold vs. slide recording
- **Work offline** - Don't worry about internet connectivity; sync later
- **Collaborate** - Invite others to share the work and improve quality
- **Check your icons** - Use the Bible icon and crown to quickly identify your projects
